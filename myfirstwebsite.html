How to Deal with Stress

Written by smaerdian on April 18, 2020

Stress is a dreadful part of our every day lives

If not managed properly it can have serious effects on your mental and physical health.

Here are some ways you can reduce the stress in your life:

  1. Meditation

    2. Meditation is one of the most common practices for stress reduction.

    Deep breathing and clearing the mind helps you feel more relaxed.

    One study performed in 2012 showed that meditation programs improved the quality of life for over 3,000 participants.

  2. Exercise

    3. Exercise is a great way to reduce stress because it pumps up your endorphins, feel-good neurotransmitters.

    It also increases your overall phyical health by releasing that extra stress-induced adrenaline.

    Here is a link to a Harvard study on the benefits of exercising to relax.

  3. Pets

    4. Not only do pets reduce stress but they also reduce anxiety, depression, and loneliness.

    Spending time with your pet lowers cortisol levels, blood pressure, and releases oxytocin to promote a postive mood.
